# dao-equaling-the-heavens - Chapter 131: Chapter 109: Daoist Sect Treats Me As An Immortal Talent_3

---

"Ah!"

Gu Wen frowned slightly, "Why does this sound seem excessively delicate?"

"You are a killer Daoist Skill!"

The Red Dragon dharma form, with its ten-zhang body, flew towards him, rubbing against Gu Wen as a very faint trace of spirituality transmitted.

It was normal for the dharma form to possess spirituality; it was undetected before because Gu Wen had realized it too early.

Gu Wen placed his hand on the Dragon Head, his eyes brimming with thousands of murderous intents, only to hear a thunderous dragon’s roar enveloping the Qi Sea.

He nodded in satisfaction and said, "That’s more like it."

As the murderous intent withdrew, the Red Dragon dharma form seemed as if its throat was choked, "Awoo~"

"...."

Gu Wen decided that before the Red Dragon dharma form matured, he would not let it randomly make noise outdoors, since it was a killer Daoist Skill, not some whimpering creature.

It was probably due to the recent birth of its spirituality; nurturing it further would surely enable it to produce a heaven-shaking dragon’s roar.

Suddenly, another thought transmitted from the Red Dragon dharma form, which raised its head, showing an extreme interest in the Heavenly Marrow high above.

Heavenly Marrow was originally used on Artifact Spirits. Could the dharma form be included too?

Gu Wen was somewhat puzzled but then adopted a try-and-see attitude; with a thought, a river formed by Heavenly Marrow surged towards him, which the Red Dragon swallowed in one gulp.

The dharma form expanded instantly, and Divine Skills condensed!

[Red Dragon dharma form grows to eleven zhang, Divine Skills at four percent]

The Red Dragon dharma form grew by three meters, and Divine Skills directly jumped from 0.3 percent to four percent!

Gu Wen was greatly excited and said, "Heavenly Marrow affects the imagination?"

Artifact Spirits, Spirit Forms, dharma forms, and Divine Skills were none of them living beings but were kinds of condensed spirituality. Typically, this spirituality could not be advanced through normal cultivation alone. The talented could achieve overnight what took others a thousand years to accumulate.

Divine Skills were among the most difficult to cultivate, and the only way Gu Wen could think of enhancing the Red Dragon dharma form’s Divine Skills was through combat.

But combat was not always available, and he wasn’t a battle maniac. Instead of seeking fights, it was more beneficial to follow Yu Hua in search of Immortality Pills. If successful, he could even gain an Immortal’s backing, then leverage the Immortal’s power to kill the dog Emperor and enjoy all the Spiritual Medicines in the world.

Heavenly Marrow enhancing the dharma form was tremendous news for Gu Wen.

Above the imagination are the Spirit Forms, above Spirit Forms are dharma forms, and the fulfillment of a dharma form is Divine Skills.

Divine Skills were the most powerful offensive means for a cultivator and the standard for becoming a Venerable. Only Divine Skills could contend against Divine Skills; without them, one couldn’t be called a Venerable.

He even believed that the six swords on the Jade Sword Buddha’s dharma form were transformed Divine Skills, including the golden sword light shown by Chi Yu Zi.

And regarding the size of the dharma form, if he had enough Heavenly Marrow, could he condense a thousand-zhang dharma form? Given that the Sword Pond was a vast pool of Heavenly Marrow, if he drank it all, wouldn’t he become invincible under heaven?

By then, what could that drunkard Chi Yu Zi even compare with him?

Gu Wen suppressed his thoughts; he planned to dedicate all the Heavenly Marrow to the three swords, delaying the Red Dragon slightly.

Suddenly struck by a whimsy, he infused a load of Heavenly Marrow into the Life-saving Pearl.

[Can withstand Mahayana Realm attacks nine hundred and ten times, Dao Realm powerhouses can strike fully nine times]

Just one load of Heavenly Marrow could withstand ten attacks?

Invincible! I am invincible, Dao Lord!

Which family’s life-saving Magic Treasure didn’t need a refill?

Gu Wen felt that now he could go down and give the Nun a couple of slaps on her forehead without any problem.

With a thought, he infused the remaining nineteen loads of Heavenly Marrow into the Life-saving Pearl; perhaps the dharma form was important, but life-saving measures could not be taken lightly.

[Can withstand Mahayana Realm attacks one thousand one hundred times, Dao Realm powerhouses can strike fully eleven times]
