# dao-equaling-the-heavens - Chapter 198: Chapter 148 Slaying Demons and Eliminating Evil_2

---

The scattered spiritual sword solidified once again, transforming into a giant sword that descended from the sky.

Guardian Yang was forcibly pressed down to the ground, then knelt on one knee under the pressure of the giant sword.

Boom!

The area within a hundred miles shook.

Hu Yuan’s face showed solemnity.

Guardian Yang possessed the power of the Twelve Level, and Chi Yu Zi’s Sixth Level Daoji was also at the Twelve Level. Logically, with thousands of years of accumulation, he should be stronger than Chi Yu Zi, but now it was hardly an equal match, and he was subtly falling behind.

It was said that this talent possessed the Golden Light Spell, known as the most powerful Divine Skills in the universe for battle; perhaps this was the disparity between them.

Times of trouble, the Dao’s fortune waning.

Hu Yuan sighed and turned his head to look at the man extracting the Demon Core of the Tiger Leader from the ruins; even with his extremely reserved personality, he couldn’t help but show a hint of coldness on his face.

He said, "The Human Race treasures the inner cores of demons, killing countless demons to seize them, yet my Devil Clan seldom consumes Cultivator Golden Cores."

"You feel wronged? This entire town is empty, who knows how many mortals died unjustly, yet those mortals did not cry out in grievance."

Gu Wen, holding a blood-covered Demon Core, turned to look at Hu Yuan, almost laughing in irritation.

Initially, seeing the town abandoned had merely given him one more reason to slay the demons; it did not deeply affect Gu Wen personally, as he hadn’t seen the people being killed nor did he know anyone who lived there.

He was not a sentimental person, not one to weep for a group of strangers.

Yet now, upon seeing it, Gu Wen felt that demons truly deserved to die.

Before a human killed a demon had not occurred, but demons killing humans was right before his eyes; now he felt too cruel to kill them for their Demon Cores as retribution.

Hu Yuan showed no shame, instead frowning and retorting, "Mortals are as ants and wild beasts, merely considered Blood Food. And humans consume chicken, duck, and fish without restraint; everything in the world feeds on something else, it’s natural for anyone to be devoured."

"So what are you complaining about?"

Gu Wen stuffed the Demon Core into his cracked mouth, chewed it into pieces, and swallowed, boldly refining it into Heavenly Marrow and Mana.

[Heavenly Marrow fifty years]

Impurities in the essence gave Mana some turbulence, mixed with the Divine Thought of the Devil Clan, stirring up bundles of irritability.

His Chopping Clouds Sword in hand pointed at Hu Yuan, Primordial Spirit locked on, Sword Intent crushing.

"The law of the jungle, it’s natural for me to devour you."

Hu Yuan felt as if plunged into an ice cave for an instant, a shadow enveloping him, a Taoist Sword emanating cold light was raised high, its ordinary facade diffused with the supreme brilliance of a sword light.

Clang!

Five Level Four Level fulfilled, yet somehow it seemed comparable to the Twelve Level heavens.

Hu Yuan managed to condense his dharma form in half a breath’s time, revealing the aspect of the Nine-Tailed Sky Fox, Six Level Five Level fulfilled, two levels higher than Gu Wen.

It was almost a subconscious action, even he found it unbelievable. He was two levels higher than his opponent, both prodigies without the concept of surmounting levels, as they were walking the path of extremities.

Within the same realm, there might be a difference in combat strength, but not as significant as between fulfilled and unfilled.

Gu Wen’s indifferent voice sounded.

"Or are you scared?"

A sword swung down, cutting through half of the Sky Fox dharma form. In a crucial moment, Hu Yuan barely dodged a fraction, yet he still saw his own blood spraying into the sky, an arm flying in mid-air, each drop of blood radiant under the sword light.

How is this possible?!

Hu Yuan’s breath halted, he looked disbelievingly at the surroundings that seemed to slow down countless times.

He was two levels higher than his opponent, not falling behind in Mana or dharma form, yet his arm was still severed by a sword.

Time waits for no demon; thoughts couldn’t clarify the difference in strength in that instant, while the remnants of the sword light already tore through the ground, splitting a hundred meters in an instant, and instantly, three or four houses collapsed.

Gu Wen, two levels behind in strength, Five Level Round Fulfilment indeed, but there were still differences within fulfillment.

Yuqing Daoji Human Immortal Skill, True Sword Dao, Nine Revolutions Golden Pill, three Immortal Skills converging in one, stacking up made Gu Wen above prodigies.

Fulfillment was still insufficient to be regarded as equals before him, Hu Yuan misjudged his own standing, he was the challenger.

"Your Highness!!!"

Guardian Yang’s hysterical yell pulled Hu Yuan back to reality, followed by another dazzling sword light striking down, no fancy moves, just a plain swing.

True Sword Dao, return to truth, requiring no sword moves, those who could withstand it couldn’t rely on moves to harm others, and conversely, there was no need for those who couldn’t.

Block!

Suddenly countless pale pink petals burst forth from Hu Yuan, clashing with the Chopping Clouds Sword, the True Sword Dao’s sword light surprisingly unable to instantly sever all petals, sparks erupting from the friction.

Gu Wen finished with one sword, his arm muscles contracted, and he swung out with three swords in an extreme effort, one instant, three slashes with the sword light.

Hu Yuan, protected by the petals, flew backward, crashing into the ruins.

He wasn’t dead.

Gu Wen pondered internally, his figure already rushing forward, only ten steps out he sensed something amiss and cautiously stopped.

From within the ruins, pale red petals drifted endlessly, flower rain began to fall from the sky.

A jade hand slowly parted the sea of flowers, a graceful figure emerged showing a radiant face, as beautiful as water, eyes like autumn waves.

"True Sword Dao, truly the strongest Attack and Conquest Technique between heaven and earth."

Mother’s?

Gu Wen paused in his actions, his face showing confusion, observing the beautiful woman before him, possessing nine tails.

Such a posture made the enchanting woman delicately smile, slightly bowing in greeting, her voice seductive, "My name is Tushan Xue, pleased to meet you, Brother."
