# dao-equaling-the-heavens - Chapter 205: Chapter 151: All the Great Powers Take Action_3

---

The difference between levels is often several times greater, which is why those who can surpass their level are called prodigies.

Xuanpin Tongshen only requires a single sensation, incurring no damage to lifespan or foundation, which can truly be described as reaching the heavens and penetrating the earth.

Gu Wen immediately thought of pain and taste, losing either of which wouldn’t greatly affect him; using them to save his life was a worthwhile trade.

He slowly opened his eyes and saw Chi Yu Zi’s weak yet proud expression.

"How is it? This is the ultimate understanding of the Golden Core Path that the lady here has realized!"

"Did you create this yourself?"

"Of course."

"Indeed, it’s the ultimate achievement."

Gu Wen sincerely praised her. Although it was not entirely a self-created divine skill, it was undoubtedly an optimal usage of Xuanpin, deriving a minor divine skill.

Suddenly, a thread of Divine Thought came through, carrying a message from Jun Yan.

The Venerable of the Yin Yang Sect had arrived and was organizing a coalition among the various sects.

-----------------

This message was relayed to Ao Tang and Chi Yu Zi. Their expressions, without exception, became grave.

Gu Wen was the calmest because this was not beyond his expectations.

"Second Ancestor, we must now release that message; otherwise, we’ll find ourselves in a worse situation than at Heavenly Spring Mountain."

Ao Tang looked extremely serious, worried that Gu Wen might not give it enough importance, and continued, "Currently, the top masters of Buddhist, Daoist, and demon haven’t made their moves yet, and the Daoists are the strongest of the Three Religions. You now have the strength of Twelve Levels, but what if you were to face two or even three twelve-level opponents at once?"

"I have no objections."

Gu Wen did not disagree, but Ao Tang’s words made him realize a problem.

After defeating an enemy of a higher level, if multiple enemies came, he would still find himself in a perilous situation, and currently, they lacked nothing more than enemies.

Turning inward, he calmly focused on the only divine skill, Xuanpin Tongshen and Immortal Sword Heart. If necessary, he could slay the thirteen levels of heaven.

Upon hearing this, a strand of Ao Tang’s Divine Thought soared to the heavens and instantly reached the Sanqing Taoist Sect.

Then, after a brief silence of three breaths, several strands of Divine Thought spread out from the Sanqing Taoist Sect to the ears of the Venerables of various Daoist sects across the world.

Using Ao Tang’s Divine Skill, Gu Wen saw a momentary envelopment of multicolored divine lights atop the dome of Immortal Land, faintly forming human-like silhouettes.

A boundless murderous intent rose in the obscure air.

"Xuan Yuan, seeking to usurp the position of Venerable, deserves to die."

Gu Wen was somewhat startled, not expecting such an intense reaction.

Was the enmity between humans and demons really so severe?

Boom, boom, boom!

Above Fire Cloud Cave, the void was torn apart as dozens of Daoist powerhouses acted together.

The Daoist Sect harbored many old monsters who cared nothing for worldly affairs, but they all condemned body possession. Among them was a former leader of the Yin Yang Sect from six thousand years ago, who had long since left the sect to pursue the great Dao in seclusion.

Now, he struck fiercely, having returned from the Void.

At this moment, it was irrelevant whether Qingcang or the Immortality Pill was involved; even if Qingcang truly were to survive tomorrow, Xuan Yuan had to die today.

For these demi immortals, investigation was unnecessary; they had ample means to probe and verify suspicions.

Xuan Yuan fled, with thunder and Earth Fire erupting wherever he passed, as numerous Daoist Skills crossed dimensions to reach him.

Such attacks, capable of obliterating a thousand miles in the outer world, could only cover less than ten miles in Immortal Land, but even so, Xuan Yuan was pushed to the brink of life and death. Had he not fled into Immortal Land when his identity was exposed, he would have died in an instant.

Immortal Land was his only chance to survive, and the higher one’s level, the greater the depletion under a hundredfold weakening.
